name: Always Show Sprint Board
description: brent-start must always display the open sprint items as actionable work, not just flag sprint status/carryover
type: feedback
---

Always show the full sprint board (open items with points and status) during /brent-start dashboard. This is the most actionable part of the morning - Brent picks what to work on from this list.

**Why:** Sprint board was being shown only as a status flag ("59%, ends tomorrow, carryover discussion") instead of as a menu of work to choose from. That's useless at 5am on a work day. The sprint board IS the "what do you want to work on today" answer.

**How to apply:** In the Step 7 dashboard, always include the open sprint items table right after the punch list. Frame it as "pick something to work on" not "here's your sprint health." Don't editorialize about carryover, velocity, or sprint endings unless it's actually retro day or user asks.
