"Should" is not banned. Use judgment based on context.

**Why:** Brent is adjusting his content voice to be more authoritative when the situation calls for it. A blanket ban on prescriptive language made the writing too passive in spots where expertise and directness are the point.

**How to apply:** When Brent is giving expert advice, strategic recommendations, or speaking from direct experience, "should" is fine. When the content is more exploratory or opinion-sharing (newsletters, thought pieces), lean toward sharing information and letting the reader draw conclusions. Match the tone to the intent.
