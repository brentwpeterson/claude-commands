LinkedIn article SEO descriptions must be under 160 characters. Keep them short and punchy.

**Why:** LinkedIn truncates at 160 characters. Brent has flagged this multiple times. The descriptions I write are consistently too long.

**How to apply:** Before writing any LinkedIn meta description, aim for under 120 characters to leave breathing room. Never exceed 160. Count before committing.
