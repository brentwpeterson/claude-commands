LinkedIn newsletters automatically publish a post with the link to the article. Do NOT add a [NEWSLETTER LINK] placeholder, "Read the full story" CTA with a link, or any manual link before the hashtags. LinkedIn handles this automatically.

**Why:** Brent has corrected this multiple times across many newsletter editions. Adding a redundant link looks unprofessional and shows Claude doesn't understand how LinkedIn newsletters work.

**How to apply:** When drafting LinkedIn newsletter promo posts, end with the closing line and then hashtags. No link. No "Full story in this week's newsletter." No "[NEWSLETTER LINK]". The post IS the newsletter announcement and LinkedIn includes the link itself.
