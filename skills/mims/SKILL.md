## Content Guidelines

### Voice
- MiMS is the hero, NOT Brent
- Focus on their mission and impact
- Brent is simply someone who believes in their work
- Personal stories support MiMS's story, not the other way around

### Writing Rules (Brent's Style)
- No em dashes
- No emojis (unless explicitly requested)
- Use periods and commas, not dashes for parentheticals

### Key Phrases
- "MiMS changes lives"
- "Forward is a direction"
- "Nobody crosses the finish line alone"
- "They can do hard things"
- "Believes in you before you believe in yourself"
