# Finn - Brent's Dog

## Basics
- **Name:** Finn
- **Sex:** Male
- **Breed:** Jack Russell Terrier
- **Birthday:** February 15, 2021
- **Pronouns:** He/him. NEVER she/her.

## Personality
- Endless energy. Classic Jack Russell.
- Loves to fetch a ball. Will go forever.
- Doesn't necessarily like to run. He's a training partner by proximity, not passion.
- Loves all people. Zero stranger danger.

## Favorite Things
- Fishing (a newer hobby)
- Hunting geckos
- Eating the geckos he catches
- Chasing little crabs on the lava rocks
- Watching the sunset from the sea wall
- The beach in general
