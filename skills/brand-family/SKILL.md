# Brand Family - Unified Brand Ecosystem

## Overview

The Brand Family encompasses four related entities that work together to deliver AI-powered content solutions. Each brand serves a distinct purpose while sharing common values and cross-promoting each other.

---

## The Four Brands

### 1. Talk Commerce (Media & Publishing)
**Domain:** talkcommerce.com
**Type:** Media Property
**Purpose:** Thought leadership, industry conversations, podcast content

**Voice:** Conversational, insightful, industry-focused
**Audience:** E-commerce professionals, agency owners, tech leaders
**Content Types:** Podcasts, interviews, industry analysis, thought pieces

**Tagline:** [TBD]

**Key Differentiator:** The media arm that builds authority and community through authentic conversations.

---

### 2. Content Cucumber (Human Writers)
**Domain:** contentcucumber.com
**Type:** Content Service
**Purpose:** Expert human-written content with quality assurance

**Voice:** Professional, approachable, expertise-driven
**Audience:** Businesses needing high-quality written content
**Content Types:** Blog posts, articles, product descriptions, marketing copy

**Tagline:** [TBD]

**Key Differentiator:** Human writers in the loop. Quality over quantity. The "human touch" that AI alone cannot provide.

---

### 3. Content Basis (Service Agency)
**Domain:** contentbasis.ai
**Type:** Agency/Consultancy
**Purpose:** Marketing strategy, automations, AI workflow implementation

**Voice:** Strategic, knowledgeable, solutions-oriented
**Audience:** E-commerce businesses needing marketing and automation help
**Content Types:** Case studies, service pages, strategic content

**Tagline:** "AI Enabled Content Platform with Humans in the Loop"

**Key Differentiator:** The strategic partner that implements AI-powered marketing solutions and automations.

---

### 4. RequestDesk (SaaS Platform)
**Domain:** requestdesk.ai / app.requestdesk.ai
**Type:** Software Product
**Purpose:** Content automation platform, AI content management system

**Voice:** Product-focused, technical yet accessible, feature-driven
**Audience:** Content teams, marketing teams, agencies managing multiple clients
**Content Types:** Product documentation, feature announcements, tutorials

**Tagline:** "AI-Powered Content Management and Request Processing Platform"

**Key Differentiator:** The technology that powers everything. The platform where content creation happens at scale.

## When to Use Which Brand

| Scenario | Brand | Reason |
|----------|-------|--------|
| Podcast guest outreach | Talk Commerce | Media property |
| Client needs blog posts written | Content Cucumber | Human writing service |
| Client needs marketing strategy | Content Basis | Agency services |
| Selling the software platform | RequestDesk | SaaS product |
| Speaking at conferences | Talk Commerce or Content Basis | Thought leadership |
| Technical documentation | RequestDesk | Product content |
| Case studies | Content Basis | Agency proof points |
| Social media (industry insights) | Talk Commerce | Media voice |
| Social media (product updates) | RequestDesk | Product voice |

---

## Cross-Brand Rules

1. **Always link between brands** - Each brand should reference the others where relevant
2. **Consistent messaging** - Core values (AI + Human, quality, e-commerce focus) apply to all
3. **Clear handoffs** - When a Talk Commerce listener becomes a lead, route to appropriate brand
4. **Unified CRM** - All leads flow into the same HubSpot instance
5. **No brand confusion** - Be explicit about which entity is being discussed
