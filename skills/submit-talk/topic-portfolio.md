## Topic 1: AI Discoverability and Your Brand

**Ecommerce framing:** How AI is changing product discovery and what merchants need to do about it.

**Broad framing:** Your brand in the age of AI search. How AI answers questions about you, how to influence what it says, and why traditional SEO isn't enough anymore.

**The proof:** Built RequestDesk, an AI-powered brand management platform. Developed AEO/AIO/GEO scoring tools. Writes "The Agentic Commerce Guy" newsletter on this exact topic.

**Best formats:** Talk, workshop, keynote
**Audiences:** Marketing, content, SEO, SaaS, general business, AI/tech
