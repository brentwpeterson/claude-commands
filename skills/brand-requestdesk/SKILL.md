## Positioning

**Sentence:** RequestDesk manages your brand voice and automates content creation, publishing, and optimization across every platform.

**Paragraph:** RequestDesk is the AI-powered platform that manages your brand from voice to publish. Build your brand voice, store it, and verify every piece of content against it. Analyze your brand positioning and compare it to competitors. Create blog posts, product content, and social media in your brand voice and publish directly to WordPress, Shopify, and social platforms. Pull product data from your e-commerce store to generate content automatically. Submit content requests and route them to writers for fulfillment. Host your CMS content for headless sites. Score your AEO, AIO, and GEO readiness. One platform that automates the entire content lifecycle.

## Voice and Tone

> **Baseline writing rules come from `/brand-brent`.** All rules below inherit from brand-brent (no em dashes, no emojis, no vague hooks, active voice, banned phrase list). Brand-specific adjustments below override or extend the baseline where noted.

- Mentor/guide, clever but professional
- Focus on outcomes, not features
- Client is the hero, RequestDesk is the tool that makes them faster
- Technical when needed, plain language by default

### Never Use
- Em dashes (use periods, commas, or parentheses)
- Emojis (unless explicitly requested)
- "Game-changing," "revolutionary," "cutting-edge"
- "Unlock your potential"

### Always Use
- Active voice
- Specific examples
- Plain language over jargon
